JobBoard For Freelancers, Web Developers

In our job portal project, we've developed two key modules: the Candidate Module and the Company Module. The Candidate Module is designed to cater to job seekers, providing them with a user-friendly interface to create profiles, upload resumes, and search for relevant job opportunities. Through this module, candidates can also receive notifications about new job openings that match their skills and preferences. Additionally, they can track their application statuses and communicate directly with potential employers. On the other hand, the Company Module is tailored to the needs of employers and recruiters. It allows companies to create profiles, post job listings, and manage the entire hiring process from candidate screening to final selection. Employers can search through candidate profiles, schedule interviews, and collaborate with their team members to make informed hiring decisions. The module also provides analytics and reporting features to track the effectiveness of recruitment efforts. By integrating these two modules seamlessly, our job portal ensures a smooth and efficient experience for both candidates and companies, facilitating meaningful connections and successful placements.
